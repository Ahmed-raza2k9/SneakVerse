Privacy Policy

Your data, your control. Transparent, secure, and compliant practices.

Last updated: Sep 2025

Data Security

We use encryption and limited access controls to protect your information.

Privacy First

No unnecessary data. You can request, update, or delete your data anytime.

Compliance

Follows applicable privacy laws and best practices for e‑commerce.

Information We Collect

Personal Details

Name, email, and phone number to create orders and contact you about your purchase.

Shipping Information

Address and region to calculate delivery, taxes, and ship your items safely.

Order & Transaction

Order items, totals, and status. Payments are processed via secure gateways; we don’t store card data.

Optional Data

Feedback, wishlist, or preferences collected only with your consent to improve your experience.

We collect only what’s necessary to fulfill your order and provide support.

Orders & Fulfillment

Used to process payments, ship products, and provide updates.

Notifications

Transactional emails/SMS for confirmations, shipping, and support.

Analytics

Anonymous usage insights to improve performance and UX.

Your Controls

Access, correct, or delete your data by contacting support.

How We Protect Your Data

Transport‑layer encryption (HTTPS) on our website.
Restricted employee access on a need‑to‑know basis.
Secure payment processing via trusted gateways.

Sharing & Third Parties

We do not sell your personal data. We may share limited information with service providers strictly for order processing, shipping, payments, analytics, and customer support under contractual safeguards.

Children’s Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. If we learn that we have collected data from a child, we will delete it promptly.

Your Rights

Request a copy of your data.
Ask us to correct or delete your data.
Opt‑out of non‑essential communications.

To exercise your rights, contact us at Contact Support.

Have Questions?

We’re happy to clarify any privacy concerns you might have.
